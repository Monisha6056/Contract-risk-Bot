Contract Analysis and Risk Assessment Bot
Hackathon Project

Name: Monisha

Project Overview

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) often sign contracts without fully understanding legal terms. This can cause financial loss and legal problems.
This hackathon project helps business owners understand contracts easily before signing them.
The Contract Analysis and Risk Assessment Bot analyzes contract documents and highlights risky clauses in simple language.

What This Project Does:

1.Allows users to upload contracts in PDF, DOCX, or TXT
2.Identifies the type of contract (Service, Lease, Employment, etc.)
3.Splits the contract into individual clauses
4.Detects risky clauses such as:Unilateral termination,Penalties,Unlimited liability,One-sided terms.
5.Assigns risk levels (Low / Medium / High)
6.Suggests safer alternatives for risky clauses
7.Gives an overall contract risk score
8.Provides a simple summary for business owners

Key Legal NLP Features (Rule-Based)
1.Contract Type Classification
2.Clause Extraction
3.Named Entity Recognition:
.Parties
.Dates
.Amounts
4.Jurisdiction
5.Risk & Compliance Detection
6.Clause-level Risk Scoring
7.Plain-language Explanation for SMEs
